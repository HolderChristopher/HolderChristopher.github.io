Welcome to my academic website!

My name is Christopher Holder. I recently completed my Ph.D. in September 2021 with a focus in Biogeochemical Oceanography at Johns Hopkins University in the Department of Earth and Planetary Sciences. I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher in the same department working with Dr. Anand Gnanadesikan on a recently funded NSF project that I assisted in writing. My research focuses on using machine learning to uncover relationships in oceanographic datasets. Please visit the "Current Projects" tab above to read more about the details of our projects!
